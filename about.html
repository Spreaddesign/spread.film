Partners to make films that spread

We care deeply about researching the subject of our film/s to make a difference with the films we make. Taking a human centred approach, we make narratives and meaningful content with oodles of cinematic style.

Live, Breathe and Spread Film.

We have design roots - we are born out of a design firm, where the film discipline and love of films meets the design approach. Besides, we stay connected to the mothership, taking in the design spirit, so that we don’t just make digital content, we design films.

We engage in the entire process from pre-production, production, to post-production involving our inhouse talent, associates, and physical spaces.

Sonia Manchanda

Director • Producer

She studied design and film at the prestigious National Institute of Design. With a passion for film, photography, editing and music. She sees storytelling and film as a natural extension of design and weaves film naturally into her design work. She is a naturalist and a farmer as well, constantly seeking and finding inspiration in nature with a deep desire to make positive change possible.

Girish Raj Nair

Director • Producer

A former vice president and entrepreneur with over 2 decades of experience in advertising at contract advertising and McCann Erickson, Girish has worked on a host of national / international brands. A well-versed writer, songwriter, analyst, strategist, and design thinker all rolled into one. He is a co-founder, director, and producer at Spread Films having over the years worked with the best talents in the country.

Collective

Bhisma Pratim

Editor • Colorist

A student of cinema from FTII, Pune, an editor of various award-winning feature films, shorts and documentaries. His fascination with cinema is like a language. He has worked as a Digital Imaging Technician with the iconic Paramount Studios, Yash Raj Films, and the Academy Award winner DOP, ‘Anthony Mantle’. In his own time, you can find him doodling with knobs and patching cables to create music of analogue synths and sequencers.

Sreenath Nagarajan

Cinematographer • Animator

Shooting films is an art, a way of storytelling for him. A cinematographer in the Tamil film industry, and for various ad films, he takes on the challenge to bring something new everytime he shoots. His camera is always in movement and you could find him experimenting with his fascination with lights, colors, and shadows on set. As for him, cinematography is a field with infinite possibilities.
